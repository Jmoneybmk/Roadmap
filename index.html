Welcome to AniVerse Games!

Choose your game mode. Anime-themed party games for you and your friends.

DEBATE
Debate Battle
Draw scenarios, pick random anime characters, debate outcomes, and vote on winners.
DRAFT
Team Draft Royale
Snake draft your dream anime team, assign roles, debate matchups, and vote for the winner.
TRIVIA
Anime Trivia
Test your anime knowledge with multiple-choice questions. First to the target score wins!
VERSUS
Versus Mode
Draft your fighter, battle random opponents with unique challenges, vote on matchups, and climb to victory.
HI/LO
Anime Rating Guess
Bet points guessing if anime ratings are higher or lower. Modifiers and strategic betting add chaos!
TOURNAMENT
Crown The King
Vote through tournament brackets to crown the ultimate anime character or series champion.
TRIAL
Court Battle
Anime characters on trial! Teams argue prosecution and defense in timed rounds. Judge delivers the verdict.
HANGMAN
Anime Hangman
Teams race to guess anime characters, moves, and locations. Use hints carefully - they cost guesses!
